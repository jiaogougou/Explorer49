Wellness Oasis · Insurance
Your mind and body are your most important companions on this journey.

Here are some essential wellness tips and resources to keep you healthy!
UHIP
(University Health Insurance Plan)
UHIP is a mandatory health insurance plan for all international students in Ontario.
This plan covers essential healthcare services so you can access medical treatment when needed.

Why it Matters
This shield ensures that if you get sick or injured, you can access necessary medical care without facing overwhelming costs.
You'll pay only a small portion (co-pay) rather than the full, often expensive, out-of-pocket fees.
What It Covers
  1. Basic Medical Visits: Seeing a doctor or visiting a clinic
  2. Inpatient Care Coverage: Costs related to hospital stays
  3. Emergency Medical Care: Urgent treatment for sudden conditions
  4. Diagnostic Tests: X-rays, lab work, and other examinations
