Residential Construction · Internet
On this new continent, a stable internet connection is crucial for exploring knowledge, submitting your work, and connecting with companions.

Here is a network survival guide prepared for you.
Major Carriers
Bell, Rogers, and Telus
They are very popular and well-known telecommunications companies.
Features
  1. Widely available in most homes
  2. Easy setup and customer service
  3. Student discounts often available
Tips
  1. Internet speeds may slow down during peak hours (e.g., evenings)
  2. Prices may increase after the first-year promotion period, so stay alert or be ready to negotiate
  3. Speeds can be unstable in older buildings due to wiring issues
Local and Specialist Providers
Beanfield, TechSavvy, Distributel, CarryTel
Features
  1. Typically offers extremely fast upload and download speeds
  2. More personalized customer service
Some Tips
  1. Prices can sometimes be higher than major carriers
  2. Coverage is limited and not all buildings are connected
  3. If new wiring is required, landlord approval may be needed
Tips:
Make setting up internet one of your first tasks after settling in.

A stable connection will make your entire learning and creative journey smoother.

Remember
A stable connection is key to unlocking all the resources of this new land.

Get connected, and start your adventure!
